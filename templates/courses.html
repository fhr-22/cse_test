Courses

The department offers the following courses :

  • Master of Computer Application
  • Bachelor of Technology (CSE)
  • Master of Technology (CSE)
  • Master of Science (Computer Science)
  • Master of Science (Data Science)
  • Ph.D. (Computer Science and Engineering)

Master of Computer Application

  • Duration : 2 years (4 semesters)
  • Intake : 30 students
  • Admission Process : Through JECA exam
  • Eligibility : BCA/B.Sc. or equivalent degree with Mathematics in all levels of education.

Bachelor of Technology (CSE)

  • Duration : 4 years (8 semesters)
  • Intake : 30 students
  • Admission Process : Through WBJEE exam
  • Eligibility : 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.

Master of Technology (CSE)

  • Duration : 2 years (4 semesters)
  • Intake : 18 students
  • Admission Process : Through written test and/or interview. GATE score is preferred.
  • Eligibility : B.E./B.Tech./MCA/M.Sc. or equivalent degree in Computer Science & Engineering or related fields.

Master of Science (CSE)

  • Duration : 2 years (4 semesters)
  • Intake : 20 students
  • Admission Process : Through written test.
  • Eligibility : BCA/B.Sc.(Hons) in Computer Science.

Master of Science (Data Science)

  • Duration : 2 years (4 semesters)
  • Intake : 35 students
  • Admission Process : Through written test and/or interview.
  • Eligibility : B.Sc.(Hons) or equivalent degree in any discipline with Mathematics as a subject in each level of education.
